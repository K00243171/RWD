Nando's is Coming To Limerick!

The popular south African restaurant chain is bringing it's food to the Crescent Shopping Centre.

Shoppers begin queuing up at 3am outside Penneys

Hundreds of eager shoppers queue outside Limerick Penneys from 3am

Hundreds queued outside the store, along O’Connell Street and onto Honan’s Quay, throughout the morning.
Tilly Curtin, 17, from Rhebogue, queued outside from the chain from 3 o'clock in the morning.
She said: “I’ve had no sleep. I came out of work at 9.30pm last night, and the first thing I thought of was Penneys. Ms Curtin and a relative “took off straight” to Penneys, where they stood all night “starving and not being able to go to the toilet”.
Michelle Timoney and her daughter Abi, 12, and a friend travelled over 40km from Ennis, Co Clare to get a spot in the queue.
