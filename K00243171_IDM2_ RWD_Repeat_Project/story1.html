Community outraged at local dumping spot

Resident of Ballynanty 'fed up' over local dumping grounds

Residents of Ballynanty are petitioning for the county council to clean up and prevent unauthorized dumping in their comminty.
Reports from locals say that the rubbish is drawing rats and mice into the nearby fields and into their back gardens.
One mother recounts how one morning, while walking her toddler to creche, she had to pass through a side alley with ripped nappies all over the footpath and a number of used needles discarded in a corner.
As it stands, the petion has over 2000 signatures, with local TD's also pitching in.
Local TD Maurice Quinlivan says "It's shocking how long the county council have let this carry on for, something has to be done, and soon."
